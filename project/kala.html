Oru Kanniyum Moonu Kalavaanikalum (English: A virgin and three thieves), is a 2014 Tamil romantic comedy film directed by Chimbu Deven, and starring Arulnithi, Bindu Madhavi, Ashrita Shetty and Bagavathi Perumal. The music comprises three tracks composed by Sankaran Natarajan, while S R Kathir handles the camera. The film has been shot in Chennai, in and around Saidapet, Anna Nagar, Besant Nagar and Royapuram.
The movie released on 4th April 2014 with Positive reviews from critics and audiences.
